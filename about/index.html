My Story

I didn't start in insurance. I spent years in the culinary industry, working in professional kitchens and building a career around hard work, attention to detail, and taking care of people. That foundation — the drive to serve, the discipline, the genuine care for others — is exactly what I bring to my clients today.

When I made the transition to health insurance, I saw something that frustrated me: most people were overpaying for coverage that didn't even serve them well. They were stuck in narrow-network plans, confused by marketplace jargon, and had no idea that better options existed outside the public exchanges.

I became an independent agent because I believe everyone deserves someone in their corner who will shop the entire market on their behalf — not just sell whatever one company tells them to push. When you work with me, you get honest advice, a real person who picks up the phone, and a plan that's actually built around your needs.

I live right here in Brevard County with my wife, our four daughters, and a small pack of dogs who pretty much run the house. When I'm not helping clients find better coverage, you'll find me spending time with family, enjoying the Florida outdoors, or still cooking — old habits die hard.

## What Howell Health Insurance Group Does

I shop top-rated national and regional carriers in your state to find private-sector PPO rates that aren't listed on public exchanges. Most people only know about health insurance through HealthCare.gov — but an entirely separate market of private PPO plans exists, and most people never hear about them unless they work with an independent agent who has access to both markets.

### Services I Offer
- **Private PPO Health Plans** — Nationwide coverage, broader networks, often lower premiums
- **ACA / Marketplace Plans** — Plans with potential premium subsidies based on income
- **Dental & Vision** — Standalone or bundled plans for routine and preventive care
- **Supplemental & Critical Illness** — Cash benefits for cancer, heart attack, stroke, accidents
- **Income Protection** — Short-term disability replacing 50-70% of your paycheck

### Services I Do NOT Offer
- Life insurance
- Hearing insurance
- Medicare plans

## The Value Proposition

### Independent, Not Captive
Most insurance agents are "captive" — they work for one carrier and can only sell that carrier's products. I'm independent, contracted with multiple top-rated national and regional carriers across the marketplace and private markets. That means I see the full picture and can show you options a captive agent literally cannot.

### Zero Cost to You
My services are completely free. The insurance carrier pays my commission — you pay the exact same premium whether you buy directly from a carrier, through HealthCare.gov, or through me. There is no cost, no fee, no markup. Ever.

### Florida-Based, Nationwide Reach
I'm based right here in Brevard County, but I'm licensed across 30 states. Whether you're local or across the country, I can help.

### Year-Round Support
I don't disappear after you enroll. I'm available year-round for claims questions, network issues, plan changes, and anything else that comes up.

## Florida — Statewide Overview

### Market Overview
Florida's health insurance market includes multiple major national and regional carriers. The average cost of a Silver-tier ACA marketplace plan in Florida is **$652/month** for an individual in 2026. Private PPO plans for a healthy 40-year-old start around **$276/month**.

### 2026 Subsidy Cliff Impact
The Enhanced Premium Tax Credits from the Inflation Reduction Act are set to expire in 2026. For Floridians who earn too much for subsidies — or who will lose enhanced subsidies — private PPO plans offer a compelling alternative with broader coverage at lower monthly cost for healthy individuals and families.

### Who Benefits Most in Florida
- **Self-employed individuals:** Private PPOs can reduce premiums 30-50% for healthy self-employed Floridians. 100% of premiums are tax-deductible.
- **1099 contractors:** Year-round enrollment and nationwide networks for contractors who travel or work across state lines.
- **Small business owners:** More affordable and flexible than group policies or marketplace plans for businesses under 50 employees.
- **Families above subsidy threshold:** A family of four paying $1,200+/month on marketplace may find comparable PPO coverage for $700-$1,000/month.
- **Early retirees:** Broad access to specialists without narrow-network restrictions before Medicare eligibility at 65.
- **People between jobs:** Year-round enrollment, coverage starting as soon as next month.

## Melbourne, Florida

### Community Profile
Melbourne is the largest city in Brevard County and the economic hub of the Space Coast. It has a growing tech sector anchored by companies along the Space Coast, plus a thriving community of consultants, freelancers, and remote workers.

### Healthcare Landscape
- **Health First Holmes Regional Medical Center** — Melbourne's largest hospital. Level II trauma center, emergency services, surgical care, cardiac services. Primary hospital for greater Melbourne and south Brevard County.
- **Health First medical groups** — Multiple outpatient clinics, urgent care centers, and specialty practices throughout Melbourne. Widely included in PPO networks.
- **Melbourne Regional Medical Center** — Additional inpatient and outpatient services.
- **Independent physician practices** — Covered by broad PPO networks; no referrals needed unlike narrow-network ACA plans.

### Insurance Costs
- Private PPO (healthy 40-year-old): Starting ~$276/month
- Unsubsidized ACA Silver: Average $652/month
- Family coverage: $700-$1,500+/month
- Melbourne's cost of living is moderate compared to South Florida, but healthcare costs have risen steadily.

### Who I Help in Melbourne
- Self-employed professionals in the growing tech sector
- Small business owners without group plans
- Families earning above ACA subsidy thresholds
- 1099 contractors in aerospace, defense, and technology
- Early retirees bridging the gap to Medicare
